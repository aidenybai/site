# Manufactured High School Research

In the last few years, pre-collegiate research competitions have been the go-to way for high schoolers to fill up their resumes and college apps.

This is a worrying sign. Research, especially "indie" research, has long been an integral part of innovation in emerging sciences while allowing the researcher to learn by doing.
